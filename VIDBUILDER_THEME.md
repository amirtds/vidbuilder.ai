# VidBuilder Theme

A Netflix-inspired dark theme with bold red accents, perfect for entertainment, media, and video-related content.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Primary**: `#E50914` (Netflix Red)
- **Primary Content**: `#ffffff` (White)

### Secondary Colors
- **Secondary**: `#3d3f4d` (Dark Gray)
- **Secondary Content**: `#d9dae0` (Light Gray)

### Accent Colors
- **Accent**: `#B20710` (Dark Red)
- **Accent Content**: `#ffffff` (White)

### Base Colors (Backgrounds)
- **Base 100**: `#272833` (Dark Background)
- **Base 200**: `#3d3f4d` (Medium Dark)
- **Base 300**: `#4d4f5c` (Light Dark)
- **Base Content**: `#d9dae0` (Text Color)

### Neutral Colors
- **Neutral**: `#2e2f3a` (Dark Neutral)
- **Neutral Content**: `#e5e6eb` (Light Neutral)

### Semantic Colors
- **Info**: `#5b9dd9` (Blue)
- **Success**: `#7dd956` (Green)
- **Warning**: `#e6c84d` (Yellow)
- **Error**: `#d94d5b` (Red)

### Design Properties
- **Border Radius**: `16px` (Rounded corners)
- **Color Scheme**: Dark
- **Prefers Dark**: Yes

## Best Use Cases

### ✅ Perfect For:
- **Video platforms and streaming services**
- **Entertainment and media companies**
- **Film production and video editing tools**
- **Content creator platforms**
- **Movie and TV show promotions**
- **Gaming and esports content**
- **Bold, attention-grabbing marketing videos**

### ❌ Not Recommended For:
- Corporate/business presentations (use `corporate` or `business` instead)
- Educational content (use `light` or `pastel` instead)
- Healthcare or medical content
- Financial services
- Minimalist designs (use `lofi` or `wireframe` instead)

## Design Inspiration

The VidBuilder theme is inspired by Netflix's iconic brand identity:
- **Bold red primary color** for calls-to-action and emphasis
- **Dark backgrounds** for cinematic feel and reduced eye strain
- **High contrast** for readability and visual impact
- **Rounded corners** for modern, friendly appearance
- **Clean typography** with system fonts

## Color Psychology

- **Red (#E50914)**: Energy, passion, excitement, urgency
- **Dark backgrounds**: Premium, cinematic, focused
- **High contrast**: Professional, modern, accessible

## Accessibility

- ✅ High contrast ratios for text readability
- ✅ WCAG AA compliant color combinations
- ✅ Dark mode optimized for reduced eye strain
- ✅ Clear visual hierarchy with semantic colors
